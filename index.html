I am a geospatial developer with phd in geomatics engineering. My areas of specialisation are location based services and vehicle navigation. I also interest in sustainable energy and environmental sciences, and always open to challenging endeavours.

I love reading books and watching videos/documentaries about development in sciences, trending technology, psychology, social science, personality development, religion, environmental sciences, history, and many other topics. This blog is an effort to archive all highly useful and though provoking contents I come across on a daily basis with corresponding short synopsis in an informal fashion. It's primarily for my own archieveing, but I hope readers will also find some of them advantageous for their needs.
